I am a molecular biologist and geneticist with solid research experience and a programming background.

After working as a computational biologist and data scientist at a rare disease research laboratory and at OmicEra Diagnostics, respectively, I am currently employed at Exact Sciences as a Researcher and Data Scientist.

The ultimate goal of my work is to combine the power of computation with mysterious biological questions to uncover the unknown.

So, let's continue 🧬 debugging DNA software!
